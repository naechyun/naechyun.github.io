Research Interests

I'm in general interested in computational genomics. I work on methods to improve sequence alignment and reduce reference bias using population genomic data. I study alignment-free methods for genomic sequnece data. I develop computational methods to biological and medical questions. I'm also experienced in designing hardware architectures based on FPGA or ASIC to accelerate computing.
